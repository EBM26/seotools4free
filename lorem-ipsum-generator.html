About this tool

Lorem ipsum is scrambled, nonsensical Latin-derived text used as a stand-in for real content, in mockups, layout tests, or anywhere you need to see how a design handles text without waiting on final copy. Its meaninglessness is the point, it keeps a reviewer's attention on layout and spacing instead of reading the words.

Paragraphs, sentences, or words

Paragraphs are best for testing body copy and line-wrapping, sentences work well for short blocks like cards or callouts, and words are useful for testing tight spaces like buttons, labels, or table cells.

Why it's not random gibberish

The classic lorem ipsum passage comes from a scrambled section of a 1st-century BC Latin text by Cicero. It stuck around because it approximates the letter and word-length distribution of real language well enough to give a realistic sense of how a block of text will look, unlike a repeated placeholder phrase.
